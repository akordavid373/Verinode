# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors. Please follow these standards:

### ‚úÖ Positive Behaviors

- **Be respectful and inclusive**: Treat all individuals with respect and consideration
- **Be collaborative**: Work together to solve problems and build the best possible solution
- **Be helpful**: Provide constructive feedback and assistance to others
- **Be patient**: Remember that everyone has different levels of experience and expertise
- **Be professional**: Maintain a professional and courteous demeanor in all interactions

### ‚ùå Unacceptable Behaviors

- **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, sexual harassment, or bullying
- **Discrimination**: Discriminatory language, jokes, or behavior based on personal characteristics
- **Spam**: Excessive self-promotion, off-topic content, or repetitive messages
- **Personal attacks**: Insults, ad hominem attacks, or personal criticism
- **Disruptive behavior**: Intentionally disrupting discussions or meetings
- **Privacy violations**: Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to:

- **Email**: conduct@verinode.org
- **GitHub**: @jobbykingz (project maintainer)
- **Discord**: Private message to moderators

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior will result in:

1. **First offense**: Private warning and education
2. **Second offense**: Temporary suspension (1-7 days)
3. **Third offense**: Permanent ban from the community

### Appeal Process

If you believe you've been unfairly sanctioned, you may appeal to:
- **Email**: appeal@verinode.org
- **GitHub**: Create a private issue with the maintainers

## Community Guidelines

### Communication Channels

- **GitHub Issues**: For bug reports and feature requests
- **GitHub Discussions**: For general questions and community discussion
- **Discord**: For real-time chat and community building
- **Email**: For private matters and security concerns

### Contributing Guidelines

- Follow the [Contributing Guide](CONTRIBUTING.md)
- Use issue templates for bug reports and feature requests
- Write clear, descriptive commit messages
- Include tests for new functionality
- Document your changes

### Code Review Process

- Be constructive and specific in your feedback
- Focus on the code, not the person
- Explain your reasoning and provide alternatives
- Be open to feedback on your own work

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All public and private community spaces
- GitHub repositories, issues, and pull requests
- Discord server and other communication platforms
- In-person events and meetups
- Private communications related to the project
